Our Story

Every bottle of Eden Reverie begins with a single walk through the gardens of the French Riviera at first light. It is in those quiet, dew-drenched moments that the true character of the gardenia reveals itself — delicate, radiant, and wholly unforgettable.

"I wanted to bottle that feeling. The warmth of sunlight on white petals, and the sweetness of something completely pure."

Eden Reverie is more than a fragrance. It is an invitation to pause. To feel the softness of the world. To carry a bloom with you, wherever you go.

Explore the Scent Notes

Scent Notes

A carefully composed floral accord, layered from the first breath to the lasting warmth of the day.

  • Top Notes

    A bright, sparkling opening of sun-ripened peach blossom and fresh mandarin. Light and effervescent — the first impression of something beautiful.

  • Heart Notes

    The soul of the fragrance. Pure, luminous gardenia blooms entwined with the gentleness of white rose — radiant, feminine, and endlessly romantic.

  • Base Notes

    A soft, warm foundation of pale blonde wood and clean musk, leaving a lingering trail that feels as gentle as a whisper on the skin.
